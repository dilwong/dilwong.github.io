About Me

I am a postdoctoral researcher affiliated with the Princeton Center for Complex Materials, working in Ali Yazdani's Princeton Nanoscale Microscopy Laboratory. I did my Ph.D. work in Mike Crommie's group at the University of California, Berkeley. The main focus of my research is on the atomic-scale imaging of emergent electronic phenomena in two-dimensional (2D) materials, particularly with scanning tunneling microscopy. I also like ranting about topics that I am not qualified to talk about.
