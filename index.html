About Me

I am a postdoctoral researcher affiliated with the Princeton Center for Complex Materials and working in Ali Yazdani's Princeton Nanoscale Microscopy Laboratory. I did my Ph.D. work in Mike Crommie's group at the University of California, Berkeley. The main focus of my research is on visualizing nanoscale behavior and emergent electronic phenomena in condensed matter. I also like ranting about topics that I am not qualified to talk about.
